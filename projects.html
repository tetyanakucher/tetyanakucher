Global Gateway -
International Student Presentation Showcase

I coordinated the Cultural Experience Presentations program, formerly known as Global Gateway program. This program is a series of presentations on different countries by SFA international students sponsored by the Office of International Programs. These presentations are given by international students who share their culture and traditions, talk about everyday lives of the people, and speak from their own experiences. International students have an opportunity to share their cultures with others, while the public has a chance to taste authentic international cuisine while enjoying learning about cultures of the world from the young people who grew up there. I was responsible for the overall irganization of the events by recruiting international student-presenters, creating promotional materials and promote the event via social media, newspapers, emails, training the presenters on the program standards, serving as the event host, and resolving any organizational issues.
